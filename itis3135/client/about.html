About the Artist

The story behind the lens.

A Life in Frames

Peter Bivona is a cinematic photographer based out of Charlotte, NC, whose work blurs the line between raw documentary and high-contrast drama. He picked up his first camera as a teenager and quickly became obsessed with chasing light through the grittiest corners of the city.

His portfolio spans street photography, portraiture, and editorial work — all unified by a signature aesthetic: deep shadows, weathered textures, and an unflinching honesty that strips away artifice. Peter's images don't pose. They confront.

When he's not behind the lens, Peter is scouting urban locations, studying classic film noir cinematography, or collaborating with local artists and musicians on visual projects that push boundaries.

— Peter Bivona
